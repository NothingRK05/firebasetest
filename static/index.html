Overview

What if you had one place to easily add, edit, and remove user stories to your product backlog? Need a way to easily move user stories in and out of your sprint backlog? Welcome to Stories! The only place you'll need to manage your user stories for any project you have! For more information, visit our About Page.

Adding User Stories

Be able to create user stories directly into your product backlog, you'll be able to add user stories with all the information you'll need. While adding user stories you'll be able to input ID, description, priority, estimate, spike, status, and assignment.

Editing User Stories

Made a mistake in your user story? Want to change your status to ready? Or change who it's assigned to? No need to worry! You'll be able to go back in and edit your user stories in the product backlog whenever you need to, this will make fixing mistakes or adding updates easy and seemless.

Deleting User Stories

Has a user story become obselete? Decide that a user story will no longer be looked at being implemented? You'll be able to easily remove any user stories you add by accident or ones that you deem are no longer necessary in your product backlog.

Sprint Backlog

Want to easily move your user stories from your product backlog to your sprint backlog? Well that will be easy to do with our move function, allowing you to easily move user stories into and out of your sprint backlog.

PDF Exports

Want to have your product and sprint backlogs exported in a clean format? With the use of our PDF export functionality, you'll be able to easily export your product and sprint backlogs into a pdf to send to anybody who might need to look at them.
